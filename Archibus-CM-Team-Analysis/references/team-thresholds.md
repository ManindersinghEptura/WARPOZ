# Team Management Thresholds and Best Practices

## Workload Balance Thresholds

### Team Member Capacity
- **Overloaded**: >15 active tickets per person
- **Balanced**: 8-15 active tickets per person
- **Available**: <8 active tickets per person
- **Critical Overload**: >20 active tickets (immediate redistribution needed)

### Status Distribution Red Flags
- **"More Info Needed" Dominance**: >40% of tickets in this status indicates communication bottlenecks
- **"Under Observation" Accumulation**: >30% of tickets suggests follow-up process issues
- **"Approved" Stagnation**: >20% approved but not in progress indicates execution bottleneck

## Aging Thresholds by Status

### Critical Escalation (Immediate Management Action)
- **Any Status**: >365 days (1+ year)
- **"Highest" Priority**: >30 days in any status
- **"More Info Needed"**: >180 days (6+ months)

### Management Attention Required
- **"More Info Needed"**: >90 days (likely customer unresponsive)
- **"Approved"**: >60 days (execution bottleneck)
- **"Under Observation"**: >120 days (needs closure decision)

### Process Improvement Needed
- **"More Info Needed"**: >30 days (follow-up process)
- **"Approved"**: >14 days (resource allocation issue)
- **"Under Observation"**: >60 days (monitoring SLA)

## Team Performance Indicators

### Positive Indicators
- ✅ Balanced workload distribution (±3 tickets between team members)
- ✅ <10% of tickets >90 days old
- ✅ >60% of tickets in "Approved" or "In Progress" status
- ✅ Active progression through status changes
- ✅ High priority tickets actively managed

### Warning Indicators
- ⚠️ 2+ team members with >15 tickets each
- ⚠️ 15-25% of tickets >90 days old
- ⚠️ >40% of tickets in "More Info Needed"
- ⚠️ High priority tickets without recent activity

### Critical Indicators
- 🚨 Any team member with >20 tickets
- 🚨 >25% of tickets >90 days old
- 🚨 >50% of tickets in "More Info Needed" or "Under Observation"
- 🚨 "Highest" priority tickets >30 days old
- 🚨 Critical infrastructure tickets stalled

## Recommended Actions by Scenario

### Workload Rebalancing
**When**: Team member capacity imbalance >5 tickets
**Actions**:
1. Identify available team members (<8 tickets)
2. Move "More Info Needed" tickets (lowest effort to transfer)
3. Consider skill/domain expertise when reassigning
4. Update ticket assignments and notify team

### Status Bottleneck Resolution
**"More Info Needed" Accumulation**:
1. Review response tracking process
2. Set 30-day follow-up reminders
3. Consider escalation paths for unresponsive customers
4. Implement weekly status reviews

**"Under Observation" Buildup**:
1. Define clear SLA expectations (60-day max)
2. Weekly status updates required
3. Closure criteria documentation
4. Manager review for tickets >60 days

**"Approved" Stagnation**:
1. Resource allocation review
2. Priority clarification with stakeholders
3. Skill gap analysis for execution
4. Consider external vendor support

### Critical Escalation Process
**Tickets >1 Year Old**:
1. Immediate manager review required
2. Stakeholder communication on status
3. Closure decision within 14 days
4. Process improvement analysis

**"Highest" Priority Delays**:
1. Daily status updates required
2. Manager involvement in resolution
3. Resource reallocation if needed
4. Customer/stakeholder communication plan

## Success Metrics

### Team Efficiency
- **Average ticket age**: <45 days
- **Status progression rate**: Tickets advance weekly
- **Workload variance**: <20% between team members
- **High priority SLA**: >90% resolved within target

### Process Health
- **"More Info Needed" aging**: <15% >30 days
- **"Under Observation" closure**: >80% within 60 days
- **"Approved" execution**: >90% start within 14 days
- **Customer response time**: <5 business days

### Team Satisfaction
- **Workload balance satisfaction**: >80% team positive
- **Process clarity**: Defined SLAs and expectations
- **Escalation effectiveness**: Clear management support
- **Professional development**: Skill and domain growth
